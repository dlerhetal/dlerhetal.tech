Do / Don't

Do

  • Lead with the 3D hero on heroes, splash pages, social cards.
  • Use the 2D vector mark for nav, favicons, anywhere the 3D would render too small.
  • Keep the 3D hero on its native stone bg unless you need a transparent cut.
  • Use lava sparingly — links, the dot in the wordmark, live status.
  • Use SVG for the web; PNG when a tool can't consume SVG.

Don't

  • Don't use the 3D hero below ~256px — it loses its depth.
  • Don't recolor or restyle the 3D render.
  • Don't place the mark on busy photos or gradients.
  • Don't pair the brand with pastel palettes or magazine layouts.
  • Don't use the full mark below 32px — use the favicon variant.
