Do / Don't

Do

  • Keep the mark on stone, navy, or white.
  • Use lava sparingly — links, live status, the dot in the wordmark.
  • Use olive for section labels and rules.
  • Respect the x-clear space; give the mark room to breathe.
  • Use SVG for the web; PNG only when a tool can't consume SVG.

Don't

  • Don't place the mark on busy photos or gradients.
  • Don't recolor the mark outside the approved variants.
  • Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the mark.
  • Don't pair the brand with pastel palettes or magazine-style layouts.
  • Don't use the full mark below 32px — use the favicon variant.
