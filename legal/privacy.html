1. Information We Access

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, the App accesses financial data including but not limited to: invoices, bills, payments, accounts, journal entries, and company information. This data is accessed solely through Intuit's official API with your explicit authorization.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the financial management, analysis, and reporting services you have engaged us to deliver. We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to the services we provide to you.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens (used to maintain your authorized connection) are stored securely on systems controlled by the Company. Financial data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is processed locally and is not transmitted to third-party services beyond what is necessary to operate the App.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with third parties. Data may be shared only: (a) with your explicit consent; (b) as required by law; (c) with service providers who assist in operating the App, subject to confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Upon termination of our engagement, we will delete your data within 30 days unless required by law to retain it longer.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including OAuth 2.0 authentication, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and secure token storage. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may: (a) revoke the App's access to your QuickBooks Online account at any time; (b) request a copy of the data we hold about you; (c) request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Intuit Data Use

Our use of information received from Intuit APIs adheres to the Intuit Developer Terms of Service, including limitations on data use and storage.
